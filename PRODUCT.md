# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Linux developers, single-board-computer enthusiasts, and A733 owners evaluating or reproducing the Radxa A7Z Debian desktop stack.

## Product Purpose

Show that a modern, reproducible Debian 12 desktop stack is achievable on the A733, preserve the technical evidence, and make each hardware-enablement milestone understandable and reusable.

## Brand Personality

Precise, restrained, and quietly ambitious. The project should feel like credible engineering that earns excitement through working hardware and reproducible evidence.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative gradients, dark promotional panels, glossy cards, excessive color, generic AI-generated launch graphics, and visuals that obscure the technical sequence. Do not use color as the only carrier of meaning.

## Design Principles

- Lead with verified outcomes, then expose the evidence.
- Make technical progress understandable at a glance.
- Prefer diagrams, real screenshots, and exact identifiers over decoration.
- Use restraint to increase confidence, not to reduce energy.
- Keep English and Chinese documentation aligned.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use high-contrast text and lines, keep labels readable at README width, support light and dark color schemes, and ensure every state or relationship remains understandable without color perception.
